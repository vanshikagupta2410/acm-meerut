Initiative Undertaken By Meerut ACM Professional Chapter



1. Establishment of Sub-Society:

NextGen Wearables Hub The launch of the NextGen Wearables Hub marks a pivotal stride within the Meerut ACM Professional Chapter, aimed at embracing and exploring the rapidly evolving domain of wearable technology. This initiative is a platform designed to foster collaboration, enabling individuals to deeply engage with the applications, innovations, and implications of wearable technology across diverse sectors such as healthcare, fitness, and lifestyle. It is envisioned as a central hub for learning, discourse, and innovation, aligned with the broader objectives of the ACM Chapter to foster technological advancements and nurture professional growth.


2. Ambassadors for ACM Program:

The Ambassadors for ACM Program encourages members to spread the word about ACM among their networks. By completing the Ambassadors for ACM Referral Form, individuals can extend special email invitations to friends, peers, or colleagues to join ACM at a discounted introductory rate. Additionally, interested parties can fill out the Ambassadors for ACM Professional and Student PDF applications.


Rewards for Ambassadors

The Ambassadors for ACM Program encourages members to spread the word about ACM among their networks. By completing the Ambassadors for ACM Referral Form, individuals can extend special email invitations to friends, peers, or colleagues to join ACM at a discounted introductory rate. Additionally, interested parties can fill out the Ambassadors for ACM Professional and Student PDF applications.

- Gifts on Referrals: Upon your first referral joining ACM, you become eligible for a free gift. Subsequent qualifying referrals (1st, 3rd, 5th, and so on) also earn you additional free gifts. An email notification with a link to the prize selection page will be sent for each qualifying referral.

- Special Bonus for 10th Referral: Every 10th referral entitles you to a special bonus gift.

- Monthly Recognition: The leading Ambassador each month enters the pool of winners competing for the Grand Prize. At the culmination of the Ambassadors for ACM program on June 30, a winner will be randomly selected from the top 12 Ambassadors.

Join us in advocating for ACM by becoming an Ambassador. Your efforts not only expand our community but also contribute to the progression of computing sciences and professional growth
